006 / Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need technical experience or coding skills? +

Not at all. This workshop is designed for professionals: marketers, managers, consultants, founders. Not developers. Week 4 covers AI-assisted development specifically for non-coders. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can do this.

What's the time commitment? +

Plan for 5-7 hours per week: three 90-minute live sessions plus hands-on implementation time. All sessions are recorded if you miss one. Most participants say the time they save by week 2 already exceeds the time they invest.

What exactly is n8n? +

n8n is an open-source workflow automation platform. Think Zapier, but more powerful, more flexible, and you own your data. It integrates with 400+ apps and services. We chose it because it's the most capable tool for professionals who want real control over their automations.

Is this live or pre-recorded? +

All sessions are live with a real instructor and limited cohort size (30 max). You can ask questions, get feedback on your automations, and learn alongside peers facing similar challenges. Every session is also recorded for lifetime access.

What if I'm already using AI tools at work? +

Great. You'll move even faster. Most participants have dabbled with ChatGPT or Copilot. This workshop takes you from "user" to "builder." The gap between using AI and leveraging AI is where the real value lives.

Can my company pay for this? +

Absolutely. We provide a professional development invoice that most L&D and training budgets will cover without hesitation. We can also provide a justification template to send to your manager.
